Energy Consumption Monitor

About the Energy Monitor Grid

An Energy Monitor Grid is a system used to track, manage, and optimize energy consumption within a specific area. It incorporates smart meters, sensors, and data analytics to monitor electricity usage, detect inefficiencies, and provide real-time insights. This helps individuals and industries reduce costs and promote sustainable energy consumption.

How to Use the Dashboard

- The graph shows real-time energy usage in Germany and the Philippines.
- Hover over the data points to see precise values.
- Click on the legend items to toggle specific datasets on or off.
- Use the dark mode and language buttons for better usability.
